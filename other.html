Poor Dan is in a Droop

story/animation

Poor Dan is in a Droop is an interactive comic. The assignment given to our team was to explain a complex topic using this specific medium. Our topic was the bidirectional flow of time. There were many lessons learned during the development of this project, but the noteworthy ones include, conceptualization, scope, and teamwork. Although I wish I was prouder of the final product, I can at least say that the story itself represents the topic accurately while retaining a genuine, human message that people could benefit from hearing.
