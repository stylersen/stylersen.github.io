About Ty

Ty Sorensen is a student at Boise State University. He is currently studying Games, Interactive Media, and Mobile(GIMM). There was always an interest in anything 3D, but it wasn't until 2022 that he thought of it as a career. The combination of having prior interest in 2D animation, leaving home, and watching the various high-profile 3D animation projects of the 2020's led to an artistic epiphany of sorts. He now has a newfound passion for not only consuming art of all mediums, but creating it as well. He has been honing his craft ever since. He boasts a strong work ethic, a love for teamwork, and an intense desire for knowledge. As of now, he is experienced in the following programs: Blender, Premiere, Photoshop, and Unity. His plans for future proficiencies include: Maya, Zbrush, After Effects, and Unreal Engine. He is currently looking for an internship in the field of 3D animation, video editing, or motion graphics.

Poor Dan is in a Droop

story/animation

Poor Dan is in a Droop is an interactive comic. The assignment given to our team was to explain a complex topic using this specific artistic medium. The topic we were given was the bidirectional flow of time. There were many lessons learned during the development of this project, but the noteworthy ones include, conceptualization, scope, and teamwork. Although I wish I was prouder of the final product, I can at least say that the story itself represents the topic accurately while retaining a genuine, human message that people could benefit from hearing.
